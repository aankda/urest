# URest Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 19, 2026

URest is designed to run locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, sell, or transmit browsing data or personal information.

## What URest does not collect

URest does **not** collect or transmit:

- browsing history or visited URLs
- page content, copied text, or form input
- personal information or account identifiers
- analytics, crash telemetry, or advertising identifiers

## What URest stores locally

URest uses the browser's local extension storage only for:

- your global enabled or disabled state
- the excluded domains list you choose
- the local "blocks neutralized" counter shown in the popup

This information stays on your device.

## Permissions and site access

URest needs site access so it can restore blocked browser behavior on the pages you visit. That access is used only to modify page behavior.

- **`storage`** stores your local settings and counter.
- **`contextMenus`** provides the site-level enable or disable shortcut.

## Network behavior

URest does not operate a backend service for the extension and does not send browsing activity to AANKDA LLC. The official product site is a static GitHub Pages deployment with self-hosted fonts and no analytics scripts.

## Scope

This policy applies to the URest browser extension and the official product pages under `aankda.github.io/urest`. Third-party destinations such as the Chrome Web Store, Firefox Add-ons, GitHub, or email links have their own policies.
