# Urest Privacy Policy (v1.0.0)

**Effective Date:** March 12, 2026

Urest ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data.

### 1. Zero Data Collection
Urest **does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII)**. We do not track your browsing history, clicks, or any data from the websites you visit.

### 2. 100% Local Execution
All restoration logic, event neutralization, and DOM manipulations are performed strictly locally within your browser. 
- There are no background server requests.
- There is no telemetry or analytics tracking.
- We have no way to access the data on your device.

### 3. Storage
The Extension uses your browser's local extension storage API solely to:
- Save your global "Enabled/Disabled" state.
- Store your "Excluded Domains" list so that the extension stays inactive on sites you specify.

This data never leaves your device and is not synchronized with any external servers.

### 4. No Third-Party Services
AANKDA LLC does not operate any backend servers for this extension. Urest does not communicate with any third-party APIs or external services.

### 5. Contact
If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at **helloaankda@gmail.com**.
