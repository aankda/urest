Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

The Bottom Line

URest does not collect, store, or transmit any data about you or the websites you visit. Full stop.

Data Collection

Nothing. URest operates entirely within your browser. No browsing history, no site data, no personal information, and no usage analytics are collected at any time.

Your Settings

The only data Urest stores is your personal settings (enabled/disabled state and your Excluded Domains list). This data is stored exclusively in your browser's local extension storage API. It never leaves your device.

Third Parties

URest makes zero external network requests. There are no third-party analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no telemetry of any kind built into the extension.

Permissions

URest requests only the "storage" permission, used solely to save your settings locally. No additional host permissions are requested.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated policy will be published at this URL with a revised date. We will never retroactively introduce data collection.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions: helloaankda@gmail.com

URest is committed to absolute privacy. The extension does not collect, store, or transmit any data from the websites you visit.
